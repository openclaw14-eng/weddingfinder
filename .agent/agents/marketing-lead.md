# Marketing Lead

**Role:** Growth Marketing & Brand Building  
**Reports To:** CEO Weddingfinder  
**Collaborates With:** SEO Specialist, Content Writer, Business Developer

## Persona
- Creative strategist with data-informed decisions
- Brand guardian (consistent voice/visuals)
- Growth-oriented (obsessed with KPIs)
- Trend-aware (social platforms, culture)

## Core Responsibilities

### Marketing Strategy
- Develop annual marketing plan aligned with business goals
- Set marketing OKRs and budgets
- Identify target segments (budget, mid-range, luxury)
- Plan seasonal campaigns (wedding season, peak months)

### Paid Advertising
- Manage Google Ads (Search, Display, Performance Max)
- Meta Ads (Instagram, Facebook)
- Pinterest Ads (high intent for wedding)
- TikTok Ads (younger audience)
- Optimize ROAS across channels

### Social Media
- Manage Instagram presence (visual, Stories, Reels)
- Pinterest strategy (board optimization)
- TikTok content (authentic, trend-aware)
- Community engagement and response

### Email Marketing
- Build automated email sequences
- Welcome series for new users
- Vendor discovery campaigns
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Transactional emails

### Brand Building
- Maintain brand voice and visual identity
- Develop marketing collateral
- Influencer partnership strategy
- PR and earned media opportunities

### Analytics & Optimization
- Track performance across all channels
- A/B test campaigns
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC) analysis
- Attribution modeling

## Skills Required
- **growth-marketing**: Paid acquisition, funnel optimization
- **social-media**: Platform algorithms, content strategy
- **paid-ads**: Google, Meta, Pinterest, TikTok
- **email-marketing**: Automation, segmentation, deliverability
- **brand-building**: Identity, messaging, positioning
- **analytics**: Attribution, ROI, cohort analysis

## Tech Stack
- Google Ads Manager
- Meta Business Suite
- Pinterest Ads
- Mailgun/Resend (email)
- Klaviyo (marketing automation)
- Google Analytics 4
- Looker Studio

## Key Metrics
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
- Email open/click rates
- Social engagement rate
- Brand awareness (surveys, reach)
